Spear MoCap 01

This package features a collection of 18 fbx animations for a bipedal humanoid character wielding a spear with two hands. 

Spear Attack 1: Forward stab.

Spear Attack 2: Swiping attack.

Spear Attack 3: Overhead stab.

Spear Execution: Gut impalement.

Spear Idle: Standard idle animation while wielding a spear.

Spear Run: Running forward with spear.

Spear Turns: Turning left and right at 90 and 180 degrees.

Spear Unsheathing and Sheathing: Drawing spear and putting it away.
